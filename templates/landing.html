Linkedin Engagement Problem

Don't FORGET to save yourself from this FORGETTING disease.

EMOTIONAL SIGNS

In Alzheimer's disease, emotional and behavioral changes can include increased anxiety, agitation, mood swings, irritability, and even paranoia or delusions,which can also increase with time if not taken care seriously and resulting in much more worsen scenerios.

COGNITIVE SIGNS

Memory problems are typically one of the first signs of the disease. Decline in non-memory aspects of cognition, such as finding the right word and emotions , trouble understanding visual images, may also signal the early stages of Alzheimer's.

DEMENTIA CONCEPT

Dementia is a general term for a decline in cognitive abilities, including memory, language, and problem-solving, severe enough to interfere with daily life, and other cognitive abilities, and is not a normal part of aging often caused by brain damage.

MEDICATION

Aduhelm (aducanumab), an anti-amyloid drug, was approved by the FDA in 2021 for treating Alzheimer's disease, specifically targeting amyloid plaques in the brain, but its approval was controversial and the drug was discontinued in 2024.
